Beautify and improve your land by replacing strip mines, old farm land, fence lines, pipe lines, hillsides etc., with wildlife friendly native grass and wildflower plantings. Long lived perennial stands require minimum maintenance. Contact us for a habitat improvement consultation.

The native advantage means that these flower varieties have adapted over the years to survive and thrive in your specific regional location and thus will perform better.

With habitat disappearing at an alarming rate, you can help provide wildlife with an oasis of the habitat they need to thrive. The native plants that you use can meet the needs, including food and cover.

These seed mixes may meet the requirements for NRCS-administered private landowner conservation programs, such as the Environmental Quality Incentives Program (EQIP), the Wildlife Habitat Incentives Program (WHIP), the Conservation Stewardship Program (CSP), and the Conservation Reserve Program (CRP). For cost-share approval, please consult your local NRCS Service Center prior to purchase.

We offer bulk shipping on native seed as well as planting and a host of other products and services like: spreading, tilling, fertilizer and lime spreading, silt socks, straw and straw spreading and Row crop planting.

These native seeds work great as a pollunation enchancement for your crop. We can plant these along side your row crops.
