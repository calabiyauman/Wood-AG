Woodland Agricultural Services LLC.

We Provide native seed and agricultural services to landowners for wildlife, bio-fuel, and the many government cost share conservation practices. Our native warm season grasses can be utilized to extend forage availability during the summer months when cool season grasses are in decline. We are experienced in site preparations, weed control, no-till planting, and site maintenance particularly during the establishment period. Our experience also includes herbicide selection compatible with the type of crop being established. An array of modern, well maintained, and specialized equipment including sprayers with GPS, no-till-drills, site prep and maintenace equipment offer the best possible chance of a successful seeding.
